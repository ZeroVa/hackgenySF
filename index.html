What is Hacking SF?

A hackathon is a programming marathon. We're gathering developers, designers, and tinkerers around the SF Bay Area (this means you), giving them food, a space to work, a nice atmosphere, and 24 hours of time. The goal: to create things and solve problems with programming and technology. We'll also have workshops, talks, judging, and prizes!
